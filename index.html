About Infinity

As a team that has been through many seasons of growth and decline, we aim to break the cycle of life and death by continually pushing through despite the hardships that may come.

The team born from the ashes

Born from the ashes of Kamikaze, Shinigami, and Kaiju, INFINITY is a step forward to change up what a drift team should look like. Our team, spread worldwide, from Europe to the Americas to Southeast Asia, makes us a truly international team. Our goal as a team is to inspire others to enjoy and have fun and to remind everyone of the blessings and gifts we all have and use them to the best of our abilities.
